# ZVOLEN TAXI Website User Guide

**Website URL:** Your website will be available at the domain provided in the Management UI.

**Purpose:** Professional taxi service website offering airport transfers and city rides across Slovakia with 24/7 availability, featuring bilingual English/Slovak support.

**Access:** Public website - no login required.

## Using Your Website

### Booking a Taxi

Click the **"Book Now"** button in the header or scroll to the **"Get in Touch"** section. You can contact ZVOLEN TAXI through three methods: call **"+421 919 040 118"** for immediate assistance, message via **"WhatsApp"** for quick communication, or visit the **"Address"** card for the physical location at Zvolenská cesta 52, Lešť.

### Switching Languages

Click the **"EN"** or **"SK"** language toggle button in the top-right corner of the header. Your language preference is automatically saved and will persist when you return to the website.

### Exploring Services

Scroll through the **"Our Services"** section to learn about Airport Transfers (Vienna, Budapest, Bratislava, Sliač), City Rides in local areas, Intercity Transfers, and Corporate Billing options. Each service card displays what ZVOLEN TAXI offers with clear descriptions.

### Checking Prices

Visit the **"Transparent Pricing"** section to see starting prices for major airport routes. All prices are fixed and pre-agreed with no hidden charges. Contact the team for custom quotes on routes not listed.

### Finding Answers

The **"Frequently Asked Questions"** section covers booking procedures, flight monitoring, payment methods, vehicle capacity, and service areas. Click any question to expand and read the answer.

## Managing Your Website

Use the **Management UI** (accessible via the panel on the right) to manage your website:

- **Preview Panel:** View your live website and test all features in real-time
- **Settings Panel:** Update website title, logo, and visibility settings
- **Domains Panel:** Configure your custom domain or use the auto-generated domain
- **Database Panel:** Manage any future data storage needs
- **Secrets Panel:** Store sensitive information like API keys securely
